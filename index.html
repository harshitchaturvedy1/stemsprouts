We empower young minds through fun, free STEM workshops — introducing coding, robotics, and science experiments to kids in our community.


About Us

STEM Sprouts is a youth-led nonprofit focused on making STEM accessible and exciting for elementary and middle school students. We host interactive workshops that spark creativity, build confidence, and introduce foundational STEM skills early.
